What is it?

This tiny fuzz pedal for guitar and bass is the perfect blend of awesome fuzz sounds, extreme affordability, and gorgeous aesthetics.

[More photos coming soon]

HUGE Sound

Simple, no-nonsense fuzz pedal. It has a single voice that goes from all-out buzz saw down to a light breakup (roll back that guitar volume!).

In-depth demo coming soon... in the meantime here are some quick and dirty sound clips, or for a longer form presentation with some narration check out this video from Justus Gash showing how it sounds with guitar and bass.

Ridiculously Affordable ($49)

I've gone through countless iterations of this pedal and have progressively made it quicker and simpler to build, while maintaining a singular focus on keeping the amazing sounds and beautiful aesthetics.

A raw aluminum enclosure with a heavy duty vinyl sticker are far more economical - especially when offering so many designs to pick from - than traditional powder coating and UV printing. Not to mention it simply looks more interesting, and holds up to extreme abuse.

These and a handful of other careful considerations allow me to pass insane savings to you without cutting any of the corners that truly matter.

Hand-made With Love

Support a small business and independent illustrators with your purchase.

These pedals are carefully and lovingly hand-made in the USA (Lockport, IL).

The illustrations come from a variety of artists around the world. I encourage you to check out their websites, and buy prints and other products featuring their work. If you build a custom pedal you'll also have the option to buy an extra faceplate sticker and have 100% added to the artist's royalty.
