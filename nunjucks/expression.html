Desktop Slider Expression & Volume Pedal

Tweak your favorite settings from the comfort of your desktop, mic stand, or anywhere within reach

Overview

Use this as either an expression pedal or volume pedal, from the convenience of your desktop, mic stand, or anywhere within reach. NOTE: These should be used with your hands, not feet!

Expression Pedal Usage

Use a stereo (TRS) cable in the top jack to remotely control your other favorite pedals. This will not work well with Eventide, Line 6, or others that use mono TS cables.

Volume Pedal Usage

This optional add-on allows you to use the slider to adjust the volume from your pedal chain, or within a series effects loop (FX loop) of your favorite tube amp. Plug mono (TS) cables into the side input/output jacks then use the slider to bring your signal down to a better level. NOTE: The linear taper of the potentiometer makes it feel a little more sensitive on the lower half of the slider.

100k or 10k?

Available in either 100k or 10k, both with a linear response (B100k and B10k). 100k potentiometers work well with most EQD, Strymon, EHX, Matthews Effects, and more. 10k is also available for Boss (and perhaps other) pedals. Please check with your pedal's manual or manufacturer for compatibility before purchasing.
