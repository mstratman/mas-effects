What is it?

A feature-filled, compact, extensible rig for testing and prototyping DIY guitar pedals or other circuitry.

  • Test pedal PCBs before boxing them
  • Hook up your breadboard to your guitar, amp, and power supplies
  • You don't even need to hook up the guitar or amp, actually, with the sample player and tone generator add-on layer, and the speaker and amplifier add-on layer.

Customize it. Add custom or off-the-shelf layers to make it your own.
