Help

Mimir's Well gives you 7 great built-in programs (each program is an effect pedal), and supports 2 banks of 8 programs each. That's effectively 23 different pedals you can choose between.

If you want even more, you can build more and simply unscrew the back of the pedal and swap out pairs of EEPROMs to change banks.

  1. Choose your programs in sets of 8 (or fewer)
    • Choose sets of my favorites with pre-made banks - and optionally swap out whatever you want
    • Choose from the curated list of programs below, or
    • Write in additional programs from the master list here: https://mstratman.github.io/fv1-programs/
  2. Copy the contents of the "your banks" text box and include it when asked during checkout while purchasing the pedal or extra EEPROM banks. You can also email mark@mas-effects.com after purchasing and send me your program list.

Alternatively, feel free to email me at mark@mas-effects.com and tell me what you're looking for and I can help you put together your banks.
